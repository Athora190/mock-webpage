Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931-2015

Leonard Nimoy was an actor, author, director, singer, and photographer. His most well know role was that of Spock in the star trek fanchise. The character is a half-vulcan half-human star sleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the USS Enterprise, they explore the far reaches of space. Leonard portrayed Spock fom 1964, in Star Trek: The Original Series to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into Darkness.
